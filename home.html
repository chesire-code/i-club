What is I-club ?

I-club   is a website aimed at making clubs in schools more active as well as very beneficial both to the school and the students. It is a club-management platform as well as a vast communication platform where a club can be able to communicate to all clubs which have accounts in the website. We are aimed at seeing club members contributing to host a competition, invest or even raise money to help the needy. We believe that the power of activity of clubs in schools can help make Kenya a better place, boost the skills and talents of students and give them some experience before finishing school.



why use I-club ?

I-club   provides a good club-management system for your club and an efficient communication platform. It enables club members to raise money to host a competition or invest and the money gotten from the activity be shared to each member according to the ratio of contributions of each member. It is a very handy website when it comes to communication; if a club wants to host a competition, it simply sends invite to clubs which will participate and even when the club wants to host a big competition then the club can post to public and any club in the country that would want to participate shall simply accept the invite. The website comes with a calendar that shall be showing you the events you have accepted to participate in.
