i-club ?


I-club   is a website aimed at making clubs in schools more active as well as very beneficial both to the school and the students. It is a club-management platform as well as a vast communication platform where a club can be able to communicate to all clubs which have accounts in the website. We are aimed at seeing club members contributing to host a competition, invest or even raise money to help the needy. We believe that the power of activity of clubs in schools can help make Kenya a better place, boost the skills and talents of students and give them some experience before finishing school.
